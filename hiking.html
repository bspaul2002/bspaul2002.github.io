Where Brooke Has Hiked

Mt. Hollywood Trail

This trail was very hard for me because it was really hot outside so I got pretty burnt. It is very easy to get dehydrated if you are out in the sun for long, so I had to make sure I was drinking enoigh water throughout the trail.

Mount Hollywood Trail is a 5.3 mile trail in Los Angeles, California. It is mostly uphill and is rated as moderately difficult.

Fairyland Loop Trail

This trail was of my favorite trails. The views were breakthtaking, it felt like I was on mars! However, it was a little but hard because the gravel was slippery to walk on. This trail also had no shade, so I had to be careful with the sun burning my skin.

Fairyland Loop Trail is a 7.8 mile trail in Bryce Canyon National Park in Utah. It is rated as moderately difficult.

Angels Landing Trail

This trail was pretty scary. At times, it was very narrow. The narrowest portion of the trail was only a few feet wide. My legs were burning at the end of this trail and the adrenaline rush was crazy!

Angels Landing Trail is a 4.4 mile trail located in Zion National Park in Utah. The trail features a river and is rated as difficult.
