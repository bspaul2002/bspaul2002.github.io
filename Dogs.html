Brooke's Dogs

Oreo

Oreo is a Shih-Poo, which is a mix of a Shih Tzu and a Poodle. Oreo is 11 years old, and his birthday is on January 17th. Some fun facts about Oreo are that his tongue is too big for his mouth and he snores very loudly when he sleeps.

Oreo can always be seen with a toy in his mouth. His favorite toy is a duck squeaky toy that he has had since he was a baby. Oreo loves to eat treats, and his favorite food is cheese.

Rusty

Rusty is a Shih-Tzu, an Asian toy dog breed originating from Tibet. Rusty is 9 years old, and his birthday is on December 19th. Some fun facts about Rusty are that he is scared of Amazon Alexa and he loves to wear sweaters in the winter.

Rusty does not like toys at all. He loves to curl up on the couch and nap all day. Rusty also loves vegetables, specifically carrots and lettuce.

Brody

brody is a Mini Goldendoodle, which is a mix of a Golden Retriever and a Poodle. Usually, Goldendoodles are golden colored, but Brody's coat is blue merle, which explains his grey color. Brody is 3 years old, and his birthday is on October 20th. Some fun facts about Brody are that he loves to steal socks and will eat anything, even lemons!

Brody's toys of choice are towels and blankets; he loves to play tug of war with them. Additonally, he loves to play fetch with tennis balls. Brody has a lot of energy and loves to play. Brody loves to cuddle and does not realize that he is too big to be a lap dog.
